Instagraham Web Application

Date: 7 April 2024

Development Skills: PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL

Overview: This project involves the development of a web application that allows users to view, add, edit, and delete photos and albums. Users can register for an account, log in, log out, and delete their accounts. They can also update their personal information within the application.

Mixue Micro Website

Date: 11 December 2023

Development Skills: JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Overview: This project involves the development of a micro website designed to promote Mixue's special offers. The application allows visitors to view event details and the event menu. They can also track the number of stickers they have collected from this website.

Smartphone Recommendation Website

Date: 21 April 2023

Development Skills: PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Overview: This website showcases three smartphones along with their detailed specifications. The ranking page displays phones according to a predetermined ranking system. Users can register for an account, sign in, sign out, and view their favorite phones.

Foodies.MY

Date: 16 April 2023

Development Skills: JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Overview: The website serves as a platform for Malaysian food lovers, offering a guide to local dining options. Users can browse restaurants based on location, cuisine type, and top-ranking dishes. The site features articles related to food culture and trends, and a search function is available to help users easily find specific dishes.

Cafe Poster

Date: 26 July 2024

Software: Adobe Illustrator

Overview:

This project explores typography as a dynamic element, using Figurative Typography to integrate type with its environment. A catchphrase is crafted to blend seamlessly, with the typography metaphorically serving as a source of light in the design.

Fairy Tale Poster

Date: 1 November 2022

Software: Adobe Photoshop

Overview:

The story of the poster features a girl who lives in an old castle and has never left. She yearns for the fairy-tale scenery outside her window. Each cute butterfly represents a dream of the girl. Now, she makes the decision to take her dreams, symbolized by the butterflies, to escape the castle and travel to the places she has long desired.

2D Character

Date: 16 December 2024

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Animate

Overview: This animation focuses on creating expressive 2D characters using Adobe Illustrator for illustration and Adobe Animate for animation. The main goal was to explore facial expressions—such as happiness, anger, and others—through simple character design and subtle animation. This work highlights my attention to detail in conveying emotion through minimal movement.

Nian

Date: 12 December 2022

Software: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Overview: This animation tells the traditional Chinese story of Nian, the beginning of the Chinese New Year. In this project, the drawings were completed using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The entire animation process for storytelling was created in Adobe Photoshop using the timeline feature. Keyframes in the timeline were used to control the position, transformation, and opacity of the graphics.
