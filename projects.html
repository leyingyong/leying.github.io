Nian

Date: 12 December 2022

Software: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Overview: This animation tells the traditional Chinese story of Nian, the beginning of the Chinese New Year. In this project, the drawings were completed using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The entire animation process for storytelling was created in Adobe Photoshop using the timeline feature. Keyframes in the timeline were used to control the position, transformation, and opacity of the graphics.
